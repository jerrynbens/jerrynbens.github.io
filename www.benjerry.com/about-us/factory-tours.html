Ben & Jerry’s Factory Tour

Jump to Section:

Visit the Ben & Jerry's Waterbury Factory and learn how Ben & Jerry’s gets so much deliciousness into every scoop! Take our 30-minute guided factory tour and learn how we make ice cream and how we put our values into action at every step of the process. It’s great for all ages, and a fantastic activity for the family. Plus, enjoy your favorite flavor – or try a new one! – at our full-service Scoop Shop. And don’t forget the swag! Bring a little piece of Ben & Jerry’s magic home with you with quirky gifts such as t-shirts, hats, flavored lip balm, and so much more in our Gift Shop. Cap off your visit with a walk through the Flavor Graveyard and pay your respects to the dearly de-pinted flavors of years past. We hope to see you soon!

30-Minute Guided Factory Tour

Tours run 7 days a week, 362 days a year. All Tours are guided, last 30 minutes and are offered (including the sample!) whether or not we are producing ice cream. Ice cream is typically produced daily although the schedule can change without advanced notice.

Same day reservations or reservations for groups of less than 10 are not necessary or accepted. Please call 802-882-2040 if you have a group of 10 or more and would like to make a reservation. The only days we are closed are Thanksgiving, Christmas and New Year’s Day.

Tour capacity is 40 people. Tour Tickets are available on a first-come first-serve basis. This means we do occasionally sell out of tours in our busier season (summer, fall, and holidays). Please plan accordingly.

Tour Tickets:
$4 Adult (Ages 13 - 59)
$3 Senior (Ages 60 & up)
FREE Kids (12 and under)

 

 
Directions & Parking

Physical address: 1281 Waterbury-Stowe Rd Waterbury, VT 05676 

We are located on route 100
1.2 miles North of I-89 Exit 10

Driving directions: Take exit 10 off of I-89 in Vermont. Go 1 mile north on Route 100 toward Stowe.  Factory is located on the left.  

Parking: Onsite parking is available in the lower parking area as well as the upper, upper parking area near the playground and Flavor Graveyard.   At the top of hill once you enter the site the drop-off area is at the end of the covered boardwalk. Handicapped, RV & coach parking are marked and located to the immediate left of the drop off area. During the summer and fall, staff parking attendants will greet RVs and coaches. There is no overnight parking available.

 

 
Flavor Fanatic Experience - Your Immersive Participation!

The Flavor Fanatic Experience at our ice cream factory in Waterbury, VT allows for hands-on ice-cream making fun with one of our employees, in our very own Flavor Lab! This opportunity is available Wednesdays and Saturdays at 10:00am, 11:30am or 2:30pm by appointment only. The experience is designed for 2-3 participants at a time. 

Reservations:
To inquire, call (802) 882-2040 or email. Please know we are not able to accommodate same-day appointments.

Cost:
$175/participant

Details:
    • One (1) Regular factory tour ticket
    • One (1) 30 minute session in the Flavor Lab– includes making and tasting but not taking!
    • One (1) Flash drive with digital photos of your Flavor Lab visit
    • One (1) Tie dyed lab coat
    • One (1) Ice cream cone from the Waterbury Scoop Shop
    • One (1) Pint Coupon
    • One (1) 25% off gift store purchase

Length of visit is approximately 1.45hrs.

Participants can direct a portion of the proceeds from their FFE to one of the below local nonprofits:

The Children’s Literacy Foundation (CLiF) is a nonprofit organization whose mission is to inspire a love of reading and writing among children up to age 12 throughout New Hampshire and Vermont.
Salvation Farm's mission is to build increased resilience in Vermont's food system through agricultural surplus management.
Migrant Justice mission is to build the voice, capacity, and power of the farmworker community and engage community partners to organize for economic justice and human rights.

Group Tour Information

We do not accept same day reservations or reservations for fewer than 10 people. Reservations are not required. If you are unable to make a reservation you are welcome to come by and purchase tickets for the next available tour time that can accommodate the number of people in your group.

The cost is $4 adult, $3 senior. Kids 12 and younger are free. Escort and driver receive a free tour and cone coupon. For US student groups grades K-12 and non profit 501c3 groups the cost for the tour is $2.00 over age 12.

To make a reservation, please call Monday-Friday 802-882-2040 or email Amy W.

FAQs
  • Can we take photos/video?

    Yes, everywhere except the glassed-in mezzanine. You tour guide will remind you when photos and video are not allowed.

  • Do we get ice cream samples?

    Yes, a sample of the day is included in the factory tour. Additional taste test-sized samples are offered in the scoop shop upon request to help you decide which flavor you would like to enjoy. 

  • How long is the tour?

    30 minutes.

  • Do we have to wait in line for the tour?

    No. Purchasing a ticket for a specific time saves you a spot on that tour. We ask that you please arrive in the tour lobby a few minutes before the start of the tour. 

  • Can I make reservations for groups less than 10 people?

    No. We only accept reservations for groups of 10 or more. 

  • Can I pre-purchase tickets?

    No, tickets are sold on a first-come, first-serve basis daily.  You can, however, purchase tickets earlier in the day for a tour time that is later in the same day, which will allow you to leave for an extended period of time and return for your specified tour time. 

  • Do we see the production room?

    Yes, from our glassed-in mezzanine.

  • Is the Flavor Graveyard part of the guided tour?

    No, you may visit it any time at your leisure. It is located up the hill, behind the plant, to the far left of the playground. It’s a bit of a walk, but worth it! 

  • Is it cold on the tour?

    No. It is room temperature. 

  • Can we take a stroller on the tour?

    Yes. You can use the elevator.

  • Is there a lot of walking on the tour?

    Not much. The tour route takes you upstairs to the Cow Over the Moon Theater, the glassed-in mezzanine then downstairs to the Flavo-Room.  

  • Can we take ice cream on the tour?

    Sure can!

  • What if you are not making ice cream the day we visit? What do we see?

    The tour remains the same, except you will not see any product or employees on the manufacturing floor.  Your tour guide will narrate a video that explains the ice cream making process. 

  • Do we still get a sample if you are not making ice cream?

    You sure do!

  • What if I have an allergy?

    Please advise your scooper or tour guide and they will accommodate as best we can.

  • Can I visit the factory without taking the tour?

    Yes. We have an ice cream shop serving up cups, cones, milkshakes and sundaes as well as a gift shop where you can find all things Ben & Jerry's.
